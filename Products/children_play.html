TIGERTURF FEATURES AND BENEFITS

  • More durable than natural grass
  • Ideal for high use or in areas with high rainfall
  • Non-abrasive, shock absorbing surface for safer playgrounds
  • The free-draining synthetic surface is playable in any weather, so energetic kids are not kept indoors because of wet or muddy play areas
  • Easy to clean and a more hygienic playground
  • Requires little maintenance, keeps costs to a minimum and remains beautiful and inviting for years
  • The design options are endless allowing to combine vibrant colours and shapes to create a wonderfully stimulating and interactive surface
