FIELD IN A BOX

The TenCate Field in a box TM connects sports with social, educational and economic objectives. It is an integrated, ready-made solution in a single container and contains a sports field measuring 8,000 square feet and suited for football, hockey, cricket, handball and rugby. It can be installed on virtually any surface while the container itself can be converted into a sports canteen, classroom, meeting place or an office. With a very modest CO2 footprint, the integrated system also corresponds to the UN's 17 global goals for sustainable development.

Our unique approach helps develop characteristics such as leadership, self-confidence, team spirit and perseverance through sport. We encourage the participation of all groups in this healthy, social activity, especially children. The Field in a box TM makes it possible to build up a sustainable and accessible sports infrastructure that contributes to social, ecological and economic change.

Come, live your Corporate Social Responsibility through our Box Full of Dreams.
