About Altius

Altius Sports & Leisure Pvt Ltd brings to India and SAARC nations the very best of indoor and outdoor sports’ surfaces that are maintenance-friendly, durable, lush and green all year through and are high on player comfort and safety. With offices and warehousing facilities pan India and an in-house team that specializes in all verticals from building to maintenance, at Altius Sports we create world-class sporting infrastructure, converting dust bowls into arenas of dreams.

Our synthetic grass sports surfaces, athletic tracks and sports flooring systems are accredited by all sports bodies. Our services include design, manufacture, construction, installation and maintenance.

We are a company with focusing on delivering a better product through excellence in manufacturing and R&D. Our unprecedented passion for sport drives our belief in delivering the most exciting surface for all sporting disciplines. In India, we have installed over 3 million square feet of sports surfaces.

With offices and warehousing facilities pan India; Altius has put its best foot forward to keep your grass lush and “green”. Our range of all-weather products are suited both for indoor and outdoor applications with special emphasis on low to no maintenance. What is more, we can even take your facility to the rooftop and create a world-class playground in the skies.


At Altius, we call this the love of the sport!
