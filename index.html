Our Journey So Far

In 2010, we installed our first FIH Global surface at GNDU, highlighting our passion for Hockey.The first multi field was inaugurated by stalwarts of Hockey in Kolkata in 2011. Apart from this, the first cricket wickets were also installed.

We built a massive playing structure on the rooftop of a school in Rajkot with supreme quality standards in 2012. The 1st unlimited yarn warranty field for football in New Delhi using natural green infill was also completed.

2013 became a testimony to another water-efficient field hockey and a host of multi installations with the civil team of Altius being formed.

The Liverpool Football Club International Academy as formed in Pune along with a kids’ playing area in Chennai along with an indoor gymnasium with lifetime warranty in 2014.

We forayed into athletic tracks with IAAF approved full pour track systems in 2015. Not only this, we brought JIO Gardens into existence in Mumbai, sprawling over 150,000 square feet for football and recreational use.

The FIFA Goal Project field was completed in 2016 at Tsirang, Bhutan; awarded the FIFA Project in Nepal. Multi installations took place for the turf a well as PU.
